Chapter 10

  • Come on, Anderson, you're just dying to see how he handles all those rigged games I had you work out.
  • Three commanders were graduating soon, including Petra, but it was beyond hope for them to give him Phoenix Army.
  • No Dragon Army in the history of the Battle School ever won even a third of its games.
  • He handed Ender his hook, the small box that commanders used to go where they wanted in the battleroom during practices.
  • Since Ender already planned to have extra practices, it meant the hook would only be useful some of the time.
  • As they sorted themselves out according to their arrival dates, Ender walked up and down the aisle.
  • Ender reminded himself that that's how he must have looked to Bonzo Madrid when he first arrived.
  • I said why does every one of you have his feet in the air and his head toward the ground!
  • None of the games looked interesting, but he played one anyway, an easy animated game designed for Launchies.
  • Bored, he ignored the objectives of the game and used the little player-figure, a bear, to explore the animated scenery around him.
